Step 1 Intake & Initial Submission

1.1

Contractor Submits Interest Form

The contractor completes the online interest form:

https://www.theclerc.org/contractor-interest-form

Upon submission, the contractor automatically receives an email outlining required and optional documents along with submission instructions.

1.2

Receive & Confirm Documents

  1. Contractor submits all documents to documents@theclerc.org.
  2. Staff confirms receipt and verifies that all required items have been submitted.
⚠
Do not proceed to document review until all required items are confirmed received.

Step 2 Document Review & Verification

2.1

Review for Completeness

Review all submitted documents for completeness and validity. Verify each of the following:

Contractor License Verify via CSLB lookup tool:
cslb.ca.gov → Check License
W-9 Form Must be current — submitted within the current calendar year.
Certificate of Insurance Must be active and not expired.
Additional Certifications If provided (e.g., LTO, Certified Arborist) — verify through issuing organizations where possible.
2.2

Handle Missing or Invalid Documents

If any documents are missing, expired, or invalid
  1. Notify the contractor via email.
  2. Request updated or corrected documentation.
  3. Do not proceed until all documents are valid.

Step 3 File Naming & Storage

3.1

Navigate to the Drive Directory

Google Drive → Operations → Agreements & Contracts → Pre-Qualification Round 1 2026 → Contractor Pre-Qual Documents
3.2

Create Contractor Folder

Create a new folder using this naming convention:

[Contractor Name] – [Date Submitted]
3.3

Rename & Upload Documents

Rename each verified document using the standard convention:

[Company Name] – [Document Name] – [Expiration Date]
💡
No expiration date? Use the date of submission instead.
Example: Smith Tree Service – Certificate of Insurance – EXP 12.31.2026

Upload all renamed documents into the contractor's folder.

Step 4 Bloomerang Record Management

4.1

Find or Create Profile

  1. Search for the contractor in Bloomerang.
If no company profile exists
  1. Locate the individual profile created via form submission.
  2. Convert it to a business profile.
  3. Add the company name and relevant organization details.
4.2

Update Contractor Record

  1. Navigate to the Profile tab.
  2. Click "Edit".
  3. Scroll to the Contractors section.
  4. Complete all relevant fields.
  5. Mark contractor as "Pre-Qualified".
  6. Save changes.
4.3

Add Profile Note

Add a note to the profile including:

  • Confirmation of pre-qualification
  • Any relevant remarks or conditions
  • Direct link to the contractor's Google Drive document folder
4.4

Verify Profile Completeness

Before moving on, confirm the profile includes:

☑
Contact information  •  Company details  •  Administrative or billing contacts (if available)

Step 5 Final Confirmation

5.1

Send Confirmation Email

  1. Log into documents@theclerc.org.
  2. Click Compose and open Templates.
  3. Select: "You Have Been Pre-Qualified"
  4. Send the confirmation email to the contractor.

✅ Completion Checklist

Confirm all items before closing the pre-qualification process.
